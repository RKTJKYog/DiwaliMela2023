What Is Diwali Mela?

One combined event of the year for greater DFW Community at Cotton Bowl Stadium - Nov 4th 2023.

Experience the grandeur and excitement of DFW Diwali Mela , the biggest ever Diwali celebration in Dallas-Fort Worth.

Join us at the iconic Cotton Bowl Stadium on November 4th for an unforgettable extravaganza filled with dazzling laser shows, breathtaking fireworks, captivating Ram Leela performances, and a scintillating Bollywood concert .

Indulge in a culinary feast at our delightful food booths and explore the vibrant Desi Bazaar. Bring the whole family and enjoy thrilling kids' rides and mesmerizing community performances .

Frequenly Asked Questions

What can I expect at Diwali Mela?

Door prizes, Ram Leela performance,Fireworks, Laser Show, Bollywood Concert, Kids Corner, Fireworks and approximately variety of vendors with booths set up - selling food and other South Asian non-food merchandise offering a unique shopping experience.

When is Diwali Mela?

November 4th 2023. 5pm to Midnight.

What can I expect at Diwali Mela?

Door prizes, Ram Leela performance,Fireworks, Laser Show, Bollywood Concert, Kids Corner, Fireworks and approximately variety of vendors with booths set up - selling food and other South Asian non-food merchandise offering a unique shopping experience.

What kind of people will be attending Diwali Mela?

People from all walks of life and ethnic background will be attending this event, with a high percentage of people from South Asian descent.

How many people are expected to attend Diwali Mela?

Approximately 100,000.

What kind of entertainment will be there for children?

A attractive kids corner has been set up with children activities and games.

Will there be security at this event?

Yes.

Can anyone sponsor this event?

Yes, please email rktdiwalimela@gmail.com.

Do I have to be a Hindu to attend Diwali Mela?

No. Everyone are welcome.

Is Diwali Mela open to the general public?

Yes

Is there a dress code for this event?

No. You are welcome to wear any comfortable outfit, keeping in mind that this is a major South Asian festival so formal traditional attire will be perfect.

Can I bring out-side food into the stadium?

Outside food is not permitted inside the stadium past the parking lot, except for baby food and baby bottle etc.

Where is the Venue?

Cottonbowl Stadium, Fair Park in Dallas, Texas

Will there be vegetarian food available for purchase at the Mela?

Yes. A variety of them!

Is alcohol permitted at the stadium?

No.

Are we allowed to bring photo and video cameras to the stadium?

Yes.
