What is Diwali Mela?

The Biggest Ever Diwali Mela, brings to you a celebration of the festival at it's best and in the most vibrant and festive atmosphere. The Mela is organized in a large open area of the popular Fair Park grounds. It is a time of joy, enthusiasm, and cultural exchange.

Highlights at Diwali Mela include:

  • Ram Leela by eminent artists
  • Bollywood Concert
  • Fireworks
  • Lazer Show
  • Food Booths
  • Cultural Program
  • Kids Rides
  • Desi Bazaar
  • Special Diwali Message from Swami Mukundananda
